What is included with my ticket?

Every pass covers all three days of talks, entry to the workshop labs, lunch and coffee breaks, and the conference swag bag. Pro and Team passes add the networking events and longer recording access.

Will the talks be recorded?

Yes. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of digital recordings, while Pro and Team members keep lifetime access to the full session library.

Can I get a refund or transfer my pass?

Refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that you can hand your pass to a colleague at no cost by letting our support team know.

Are there student or scholarship tickets?

We hold back a block of discounted student seats and a diversity scholarship fund every year. Applications open about three months before the conference on the registration page.

Is there parking at the venue?

Paid parking sits in the garage below Moscone West, but we really recommend public transit. Several Muni lines and the Powell Street station stop within a two minute walk.
